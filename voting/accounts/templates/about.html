About Online Voting System 🗳️

This Online Voting System is designed to provide a secure, transparent, and efficient platform for conducting elections digitally.

Users can register, participate in elections, and cast their votes securely. Each user is allowed to vote only once per election to ensure fairness.

🔐 Secure

Prevents duplicate voting and ensures user authentication.

⚡ Fast

Quick voting process with instant access to elections.

📊 Transparent

Admins can view results clearly and accurately.
